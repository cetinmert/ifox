Information about the iFox Beta Brand License Agreement


Welcome! We've prepared this informative article for visitors who want to learn more about the iFox Beta brand license agreement. Below, we've outlined the agreement between the developer of iFox Beta and its buyers in detail.

1. License Description and Rights

The iFox Beta license represents the permission to use a specifically developed software product. This license includes the right to use the iFox Beta software and take advantage of all features offered under the iFox Beta brand. The product is a web browser developed using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, designed especially for customer and organizational needs.

2. License Acquisition and Usage

Upon purchasing the iFox Beta license, you acquire the right to use this specially developed software. Upon completion of the payment specified within the agreement, you become the license holder and are free to use iFox Beta. As the license holder, you gain access to all features and potential of the software.

3. License Duration and Rights

The purchased license offers a lifetime usage right and does not require any additional payments. This license can be used without any time limitations. New agreements and license renewals can be optionally made, and the terms may change in such cases.

4. Intellectual Property Rights

The seller retains all intellectual property rights related to the development of the iFox Beta software. However, as the license holder, you obtain the right to use the software and its entire range of features.

5. Termination of the Agreement

Both parties have the right to terminate the agreement in case of mutual disagreement, with written notification within 4 business days after the sale.

6. General Provisions

This agreement encompasses all written and oral agreements between the parties and cannot be modified without the consent of both parties. The agreement is binding upon the successors, assignees, representatives, and legal heirs of the parties. Disputes between the parties fall under the jurisdiction of the courts in [Istanbul, Turkey].

Acknowledgment and Acceptance

This agreement has been read, understood, and approved by the parties involved. By accepting this agreement, the buyer acknowledges having consulted with an authorized representative and understanding its contents fully.
